ABOUT

Who Is Dimucc?

Michael DiMuccio, known online as Dimucc, is a Canadian-born digital creator, comedian, and musician whose work has surpassed 3.7 billion views on YouTube alone and reached billions more across social platforms.

Best known for his cinematic, story-driven sketches, Dimucc has built a global audience of over 13 million followers through through sharp writing, relatable humor, and unexpected twists. Whether it's a skit, a song, or a running bit, Dimucc always knows how to keep viewers locked in.
